Mental Health Resources

Your mental health matters. Below are trusted organizations and platforms offering valuable information, support, and guidance.
Whether you’re looking to learn more about mental health, find a therapist, or need urgent help, these resources can guide you.

World Health Organization

The WHO provides comprehensive guides and information on mental health, including tips for coping with depression and strategies to maintain mental well-being globally.

Mental Health America (MHA)

Explore tools, articles, and self-assessments for understanding mental health better. MHA is a community-focused platform offering resources for prevention, early intervention, and recovery.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Need help now? The 988 Lifeline offers free and confidential support for anyone in distress. Call or chat online 24/7 to connect with trained counselors.

Psychology Today

A go-to directory for finding therapists, psychiatrists, and mental health professionals near you. They also feature insightful articles on mental health topics.

Verywell Mind

Verywell Mind provides easy-to-understand articles and tips to improve mental health and wellness. It’s an excellent resource for practical, everyday advice.

Contact Us

Have any questions or feedback? Reach out to us, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
