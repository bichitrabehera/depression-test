Taking the first step towards better mental health is incredibly important, and finding the right support is key . Your mental health matters, and finding the right therapist can make all the difference.

Find a Therapist

Here are some websites where you can find licensed therapists and mental health professionals.
Whether you're seeking therapy for the first time or looking for a new provider,these platforms can help you connect with the right support for your needs.

Psychology Today

A go-to directory for finding therapists, psychiatrists, and mental health professionals near you. You can directly search for a therapist by specialty, location, and more.

TherapyDen

TherapyDen allows you to search for licensed therapists based on your needs and preferences. You can filter by specialty, insurance, and more.

BetterHelp

BetterHelp provides online therapy, where you can get matched with licensed counselors through a simple questionnaire to find the right fit.

American Counseling Association

ACA’s website offers a directory to find professional counselors based on your preferences, including location and specialization.
